  • Item Details

    The Innovative Percussion Soloist Series are one of the most popular mallet series available! The IP240 Medium Yarn marimba mallets feature hard rubber cores wrapped in 100% wool yarn to create clear, full tones and warm fundamentals. The IP240 uses polished birch shafts for comfortable grip and control, and is a great general mallet for marimba.

    The IP240 mallets sound great at all registers of the marimba, and are a great choice in both solo literature or educational settings! The IP240 is identical to the IP240N except for the polished birch shafts.

    Description written by Kyle Giauque
